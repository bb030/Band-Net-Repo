What is Band-Net?

Band-Net is a on-line social network that connects the different participants in the music industry. Whether you are a musician trying to form a band or participate on an existing one, a band looking to add a musician or a venue looking for a forum to promote your event, Band-Net is for the different entities in the music industry looking for a connection to create, participate or even trade. Band-Net defines the network in four kind of users: Musicians, Bands, Venues and Instrument traders. The Company aims to develop a website that will connect different instruments (melomaniacs) and Music (Melophile) lovers with the objective to facilitate the development of bands, the sharing of music, and the trading of instruments. Further, the website will allow people to sign up, add contact information, instruments for sales, Musical events, and Time availability to collaborate with others.

This project will include the following: • Documentation that tracks the completion of each sprint • A roadmap showing the progression of the goals achievement • A test plan to ensure the functionality of all the features • deliverables such as a website with a home page, an Instrument archive, etc. where musicians, bands, instrument traders, and other users will interact. Our scope does not currently cover Transactional purchases

Abstract

Music is a big industry with an estimated value in $20 billion and in the latest years have been growing enabled by technology (streaming, advanced equipment, internet, mobile applications, etc). According to woodwindgeek.com , 74% of adults in the US have played a musical instrument. These two facts are an inspiration to believe that there is a huge opportunity on helping all participants in the music industry to connect and add value through networking.

Project Band-Net is an on-line social platform that enables different participants in the music industry to connect and interact between them. The intent of Music-Net is, as other social media networks, to extend the pool of opportunities in the world of music. Music-Net will unleash the power of music to connect with a lot of people, according to Music-America.com , 52% of US households have at least one person 5 years age or older currently playing a musical instrument.
